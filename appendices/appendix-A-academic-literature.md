## 9. Relevance to Engineering and AI System Design

These references collectively shaped:
- my enterprise-systems thinking (Mertens, Scheer),  
- logistics modelling (Klaus, Pfohl),  
- empirical validation and evaluation design (Field, Brosius),  
- organisational and strategic alignment (Hungenberg),  
- user acceptance and software adoption (TAM, DOI, OSS literature),  
- human-centred design (Norman, Nielsen),  
- modern AI interpretation and data-centric reasoning (Goodfellow, Bishop, Provost).

They form the theoretical foundation underlying my applied system engineering work in  
Germany, the USA, and Hungary.
